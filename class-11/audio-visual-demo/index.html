Let's look at how we embed audio and video resources using HTML5!

Audio

    Common attributes to control playback of audio
  • autoplay: boolean; use sparingly
  • controls: provides playback controls to the user
  • loop: boolean; makes it play over and over
  • muted: boolean; mutes the audio
  • src: where the sound is located
  • volume: range 0.0 to 1.0

Video

    Common attributes to control playback of video
  • autoplay: boolean - use sparingly
  • controls: shows the controls
  • height: must be an absolute value - not a percentage
  • width: can be absolute or percentage
  • poster: puts the starting image out there
  • src: the path to the video
  • volume: controls the volume
